About us

OxSchnick is a project where Oxford students can challenge each other to Schnicks (aka games of rock, paper, scissors). You can see a graph of everyone in the university who is playing, and how they are connected. There are also leaderboards like highest number of schnicks and highest number of invited people where you can battle for the #1 spot.
This project was made as a project at Orchard! Join us if you want to work on your own fun side projects, whether art, coding, writing, or anything else :)
