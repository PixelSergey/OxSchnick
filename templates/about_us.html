About us

OxSchnick is a game where Oxford students can challenge each other to Schnicks (aka games of rock, paper, scissors). You can see a graph of everyone in the university who is playing, and how they are connected. There are also leaderboards tracking stuff like the highest number of schnicks and the highest number of invited players where you can battle for the #1 spot.
